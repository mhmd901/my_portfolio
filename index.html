About

I’m Mohammad Amer — a Computer Science graduate, AI & IoT Engineer, and Full-Stack Developer driven by innovation and real-world impact.

My work focuses on designing intelligent systems that connect hardware and software — from embedded Arduino and IoT architectures to scalable backend solutions using Laravel and cross-platform mobile applications built with Flutter.

I am the founder of HydroNova, an AI-powered smart agriculture ecosystem that integrates automation, sensor-driven data, and full-stack development to deliver sustainable and technology-driven solutions.

Alongside product development, I serve as the Co-Lead of Google Developer Group (GDG) OnCampus at the Islamic University of Lebanon and as a member of the Humanity–AI Driven Center for Innovation (HAI-CI), contributing to AI-focused initiatives and technology leadership within the university community.

As a Technology & AI Instructor at SSE Center, I design and deliver robotics, Arduino, AI tools, and soft skills workshops for both students and adults — empowering others to build, innovate, and leverage emerging technologies effectively.
